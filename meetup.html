RU Tech Meetup

April 5th, 2013 - Presented by USACS and RUMAD

Rutgers Tech Meetup is a chance for the programming student body of Rutgers to come together and demo the awesome projects that they've been working on over the past year. Come see what open source projects, production ready apps, and side-project hacks your fellow students have built in their free time over the past year. We'll also be serving hot burritos and some awesome guest speaker talks.

  • Mike Swift - SendGrid

  • Prank Roulett - Kenny Bambridge & Sam Agnew
